CYMK Industries Fire & Security

CYMK Industries Private Limited is a trusted name in the field of fire safety system integration across India, with its headquarters at Bhubaneswar, Odisha. We specialise in designing and installing fire safety systems for industrial, commercial, multi-storey, hospital and warehouse projects.

With years of hands-on experience, our certified team delivers end-to-end solutions — from system design and installation to routine maintenance and emergency support — ensuring your premises stay protected around the clock.

We are committed to the highest standards of quality, compliance and customer satisfaction, making us one of the most reliable fire safety partners in Eastern India.
