Conventional Systems

Ideal for smaller buildings, these systems divide detection areas into zones and provide economical fire detection.

Addressable Systems

Recommended for larger or more complex premises, addressable systems identify the exact location of an activation for quicker response.

Wireless Systems

Wireless systems are ideal for heritage or hard-to-cable buildings, giving flexible protection without new cabling.
