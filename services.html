Fire Safety Solutions

Fire safety is paramount for businesses of all sizes. CYMK Fire Safety understands the importance of reliable fire alarm systems in protecting your property, employees, and customers. As a leading provider of fire safety solutions in Bhubaneswar and surrounding areas, we offer top-notch fire alarm installation, maintenance, and testing services.

Scalable Fire Alarm Systems

CYMK Industries delivers scalable fire alarm solutions for industrial, commercial and multi-storey buildings. Our certified team designs, installs and maintains addressable and conventional systems using industry-leading equipment.
